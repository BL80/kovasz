Hints for baking
  • It is recommended to preheat the oven to the maximum temperature because when the dough is placed inside, the temperature decreases not only from opening the oven door but also due to the cold mass of dough taken out of the refrigerator.
  • Scoring the dough is also a separate art form, and some people excel at it, creating intricate designs and patterns resembling Michelangelo's frescoes on the bread crust.
  • If you don't wait for it to cool down, you won't be able to properly slice it!
